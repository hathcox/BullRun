## IMPLEMENTATION PRIORITY ORDER

1. **Trading Actions** (#1-10) — the core loop players hear hundreds of times
2. **Round & Game State** (#16-23) — structural pacing of every session
3. **Market Events** (#26-34) — the dramatic spice of each round
4. **Shop** (#35-44) — the between-round reward loop
5. **Timer & Urgency** (#11-15) — tension building
6. **UI Interactions** (#52-60) — polish layer
7. **Overlays & Screens** (#61-67) — presentation polish
8. **Accumulator / Tokens** (#45-47) — reward juice
9. **Floating Text** (#48-51) — feedback polish
10. **Ambient SFX** (#68-75) — atmosphere
11. **Screen Effects** (#76-81) — final layer of feel

**Total: 81 sound effects**
